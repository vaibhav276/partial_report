Training

The training module contains a walkthrough of the experience, and is necessary to understand the flow.

It is your playground to make yourself comfortable with the system. The responses in training will not be reported.

Attempt as many times as you like before going into the real experiment, where there are no on-screen tips. Make sure you complete the training for the configuration of the expriment that you want to appear for.

Start training

Experiment

The experiment module contains only the experiment.

It has a distraction free environment to focus only on the stimulus in order to maximize performance.

Hence you must complete training for the experiment configuration that you want to perform, otherwise you will have no clue about the controls, cues and flow of the experiment, and the experiment will be a failure.
